OTOS follows your team's definitions — not the other way around.

Low integration. Low disruption. High clarity.

You decide what counts as a touchpoint. You escalate — OTOS never does. If a signal looks serious, it goes to a named person on your team for a human decision. And you can stop at any time. There's no lock-in. No procurement commitment required to explore.

Minimum viable data

  • Tokenised or pseudonymised at source
  • Identity remains with the originating service
  • No cross‑organisation record coupling
  • No stored personal profiles in the pilot view

Integration posture (Phase 1)

  • No integration required for pilot entry and reporting
  • No EPR write‑back
  • No clinical record fields captured in the demo
  • Designed to sit around existing workflows

Claims guardrails

  • No diagnosing
  • No prescribing
  • No automated clinical decisions
  • No claims of outcomes in a demo context

Escalation is always human controlled

  • Signals can prompt a review
  • A human reviewer records a decision
  • Timestamped audit trail of actions and handoffs
  • Designed to reduce silent drift, not replace care
