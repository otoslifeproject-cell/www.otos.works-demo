1 ACCESS
Who can see what

Access is role-based. Keyworkers see their own touchpoints. Managers see aggregate signals.

  • Role-based access
  • No cross-org patient data
  • Audit trail on every action
  • Anonymous journey IDs only
2 GOVERNANCE
Aligned with NHS standards

Designed to align with DTAC, DSPT, NICE ESF, and NHS IG standards.

  • DTAC aligned
  • DSPT aligned
  • No EPR write-back
  • No clinical record storage
  • Human-gated escalation
3 ESCALATION
Human decisions only

When OTOS detects a signal, it alerts the relevant team. A human decides what to take. Always.

  • Signal detected by OTOS
  • Alert sent to team
  • Human decides action
  • OTOS logs the outcome
4 DATA
What OTOS stores

Continuity signals and audit receipts only. Tokenised IDs. No name. No diagnosis.

  • Tokenised IDs only
  • No clinical records
  • Audit receipts only
  • UK-hosted infrastructure
