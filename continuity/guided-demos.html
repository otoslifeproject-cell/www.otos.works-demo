ROUTE A — ADDENBROOKE’S DISCHARGE

Helen, ward nurse

A patient is being discharged from Addenbrooke's after a crisis admission. Helen uses OTOS to create a continuity thread before the patient leaves the ward.

  • One tap at the bedside
  • Journey thread created before discharge
  • Community keyworker notified
  • No EPR write-back
  • No extra paperwork
ROUTE B — COMMUNITY REFERRAL

Marcus, community keyworker

Marcus works at a drop-in community hub. Someone comes in who has been bouncing between services. He registers a touchpoint and the thread picks up where it left off.

  • Existing thread found by token
  • No name required
  • Touchpoint logged in 10 seconds
  • Service manager alerted to pattern
  • Nothing changes in Marcus's workflow

Dr Priya, GP
