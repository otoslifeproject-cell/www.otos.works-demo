How it works

A single encrypted thread joins every journey

OTOS Continuity provides a safe, anonymous way for the Cambridge ecosystem to understand how people move between touchpoints. This supports an ecosystem-wide view with minimal clinical risk. It does this without changing clinical ownership and without replacing existing systems. From this point on, this portal refers to it as Continuity.

— “There should be a single, accessible front door.” — Independent…

1

Anonymous token at first contact

At a touchpoint, staff tap Register touchpoint or scan a QR code. Continuity creates an anonymous journey ID.

No name. No date of birth. No diagnosis. Just a secure journey thread.
2

Touchpoints, not records

As someone moves through services and community support, each provider can note that a touchpoint happened against the same journey ID:

Attended. Missed. Contacted. Referred. Stabilising.
3

Silent orchestration

Continuity highlights early patterns such as repeated missed contacts and ping‑pong referrals.

It is designed to avoid creating additional admin burden.

It is designed to give teams clearer, earlier continuity.
4

Pilot reporting

Leads can see where journeys stall.

They can see where drop‑off is happening.

They can see where continuity is working.

Reporting uses anonymous journey signals.
Touchpoints, not records.
No system integration.
No extra paperwork.
No disruption.
