Slide 1 · ADHD opener

When ADHD is left waiting, addiction steps in.

People waiting for ADHD support are not being held — they are being left to wait. And when ADHD is left untreated for too long, addiction often moves in as the brain reaches for stimulation, relief and regulation wherever it can find it.

For a significant and identifiable group of adults, addiction is not the whole clinical picture. The primary condition is ADHD. The failure is not in the care. It is in the space and continuity between it.

Slide 4 · What OTOS is

A non-clinical continuity layer around existing services.

OTOS logs handoffs, generates transition receipts, monitors continuity touchpoints, and surfaces early drift signals for human review. The clinical action stays with the service. OTOS holds the thread.

Touchpoint logging

QR or link entry at the point of contact. Anonymous token ID created. No duplicate record, no extra admin burden.

Transition receipts

Handoffs become visible and auditable. Services can see whether the next step has actually landed.

Drift visibility

Silence, stalled callbacks, and missed next-steps surface earlier as governance-safe continuity signals.

Human review

OTOS never decides care. Clinicians and partner teams remain responsible for any action, outreach or escalation.

Slide 5 · The pre-pilot

A 12-week demonstrator for the people most likely to be lost in the gap.

This is continuity layer only. OTOS does not treat ADHD. It creates the receipted thread around adults with co-occurring ADHD and substance misuse so services can see whether the pathway is actually holding.

Cohort design Up to 50 adults. Formal ADHD diagnosis is not required. Inclusion is based on adults already engaged with addiction or mental health services who show clinically consistent indicators of co-occurring ADHD and substance misuse.
What gets tested QR/link entry, tokenised ID, short non-clinical micro check-ins, transition receipts, drift detection, human review, and an audit trail suitable for commissioner review.
Why the mix matters The success of the continuity engine depends on using the real-life cohort the current pathway does not hold well enough — diagnosed, suspected, waiting, or stuck between treatment steps.
What this produces Decision-grade evidence on follow-through visibility, early drift detection, service burden, and whether a continuity layer is worth scaling or not.
