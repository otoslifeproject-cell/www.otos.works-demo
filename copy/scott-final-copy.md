## Section 2 — What waiting does

Body:

It does not solve those problems; it compounds them. Sleep becomes erratic, routines collapse, money becomes harder to manage, work becomes unstable, relationships come under strain, and shame drives further secrecy and isolation. What begins as self-medication can quickly become a cycle of short-term relief followed by longer-term damage.

## Section 5 — What OTOS is
Eyebrow:
Slide 5 · What OTOS is

Headline:
A non-clinical continuity layer for the people most likely to be lost in the gap.

Body:
OTOS sits around existing services. It logs touchpoints, generates transition receipts, monitors continuity status, and surfaces early drift for human review. The clinical action stays with the service. OTOS holds the thread.

Support line:
Same person. Two silos. One continuity thread.

Guardrail chips:
- Not an EPR
- Not diagnosis
- Not prescribing
- No EPR write-back
- Human-in-control throughout

Three boxes:
1. Cohort design
Up to 50 adults already touching addiction or mental-health services whose pathway is vulnerable to silent disengagement.

2. What gets tested
QR or link entry, tokenised ID, short non-clinical micro check-ins, transition receipts, drift detection, and human review.

3. What this produces
Decision-grade evidence on follow-through visibility, early drift detection, service burden, and whether a continuity layer is worth scaling.
